Data Migration

This project's goal was to migrate data models in Cognos IBM reporting tool to Oracles's OBIEE and Power BI

Developing RPDs in OBIEE to model the migrated data, and finally replicating and validating existing Cognos reports within OBIEE to ensure consistency in metrics and calculations.
